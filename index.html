Currently the The Paradox DAO consists of the core Paradox team, advisors and investors. We will expand into a community DAO at a later date in the future.

Growth and wealth creation

Our goal is to build a policy-controlled currency system, in which the behavior of the PRDX token is controlled at a high level by the DAO.

In the long term, we believe this system can be used to optimize for stability and consistency so that PRDX can function as a global unit-of-account and medium-of-exchange currency.

Create a lasting store of value

The basic utility of a reserve currency protocol is to create a lasting store of value: a place where you can deposit enough money to buy a new house or a loaf of bread and come back ten years later and still have enough money to buy a new house or loaf of bread.

Genuine utility, however, goes deeper. It derives from use cases that extend outward, creating value rather than simply preserving it. Our goal is to take the model of reserve currency and turbo charge it through building significant utility into the treasury.
